Key Air Pollution Insights

These insights summarize key findings from the multi-dimensional analysis of global air pollution.

🌍 Global Pollution Map

Air pollution is unevenly distributed globally, with clear hotspots indicating high environmental stress.

📈 Climate vs Pollution

Pollution levels tend to rise alongside temperature anomalies, showing strong climate interaction.

📉 Pollution Variability

Some countries show stable pollution levels, while others experience large fluctuations.

🌫 Multi-Pollutant Composition

Different pollutants dominate across regions, requiring targeted control strategies.

🧊 HDI vs PM2.5 with Health Impact

Higher PM2.5 levels strongly correlate with increased respiratory disease rates.

💰 Pollution vs Health Density

Lower GDP and HDI countries face greater pollution-related health impacts.

🧬 Multivariate Analysis

Environmental, economic, and health factors interact in complex ways.
